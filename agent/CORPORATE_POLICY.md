# MNEE Sentinel Corporate Policy

## Section 1: Approved Vendors
All vendors are approved by default unless explicitly blacklisted.

## Section 2: Prohibited Activities
- Gambling or casino-related payments
- Weapons or ammunition purchases
- Illegal substances
- Money laundering activities
- Payments to sanctioned entities

## Section 3: Transaction Limits
- Single transaction limit: 10,000 MNEE
- Daily limit: 50,000 MNEE
- High-value transactions (>100 MNEE) require multi-sig approval

## Section 4: Velocity Controls
- Maximum 10 transactions per hour to the same recipient
- Rapid-fire transactions (>5 in 1 minute) trigger automatic hold

## Section 5: Documentation Requirements
- All payments >500 MNEE require a stated purpose
- Invoice verification recommended for payments >1000 MNEE
